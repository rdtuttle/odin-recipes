Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a popular Italian dish that is made with alternating layers of wide, flat pasta noodles, tomato sauce, meat (such as ground beef or Italian sausage), and a creamy cheese filling (typically made with ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese). The dish is often baked in the oven until the top layer is golden brown and crispy. Lasagna is a hearty and satisfying meal that is commonly served at family gatherings, potlucks, and Italian restaurants.
